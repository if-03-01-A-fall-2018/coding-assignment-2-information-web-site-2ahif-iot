Positive aspects

New business models and sales opportunities

The Internet of Things displaces old business models and opens up enormous chances for new services that build up on real-time sensor data and real-time information.

Operative efficiency

One of the biggest advantages of the IoT is the extraction of efficiency.
Companies use it for the automation of the business- and manufacturing processes, the remote control and monitoring of the business as well as the optimization of the use of resources.

Employee productivity

Wearables and other IoT-capable devices increase the productivity of employees.

These devices:

  • simplify and improve decision making.
  • automate routine work.
  • speed up the communication.

Convincing customer experience

From integrated customer service to personalized products and services: the IoT opens up many possibilities for convincing customer experience in both the digital and the physical World.
